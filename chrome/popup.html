Recursive Find















How To Use Recursive Find

(scroll to see more)

1. To get started, press 'ctrl+shift+f' (or click the magnifying glass icon) on the site where you would like to begin your search and begin typing in the search box.

2. If you would like more control over your search and how results are displayed, additional settings can be found by clicking the 'Show Filters' button.

3. Once you have entered your search term and (optionally) specified your filters, press enter (or click the find button below the search box) to begin your search and be redirected to a new tab displaying the results.

Filter Descriptions

Recursion Depth:
Specifies how many links deep to search for the given search term

Number of Results:
Limits the number of results displayed

Surrounding Chars:
Specifies how many characters surrounding the search term will be displayed in the preview window of the results page

Case Sensitive:
When enabled, search will be case sensitive

Whole Words:
When enabled, search term will be treated as whole word, results will only return exact matches

Search Images:
When enabled, search will also include images (note: this may increase loading times)
